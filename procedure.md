### Procedure for Economic dispatch -  

<b>STEP 1</b>: Enter the number of generators in power system. Then, click on the "Generate Generator Table" and fill the details of generators i.e., a, b, c (Quadratic, linear and no-load cost coefficients), and Minimum (MW) and Maximum power (MW) for each buses and the load demand.
<br>
<b>Note:</b> Generator data for three generators systems is already provided. To access the data click on the button of "Autofill Generator Cost Characteristics". 
<br>
<br>
<b>STEP 2</b>: Click on "Run Economic Dispatch". 
<br>
<br>
<b>STEP 3</b>: The values of the generators scheduled power and its associated cost are displayed in Table format. The total cost incurred and the lambda are also displayed in the bottom.
